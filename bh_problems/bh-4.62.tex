\begin{solution}
    By LOTUS,
    $$
    E\left(2^X\right)=e^{-\lambda} \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{(2 \lambda)^k}{k !}=e^{-\lambda} e^{2 \lambda}=e^\lambda,
    $$
    for all $\lambda>0$.
\end{solution}
